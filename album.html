Description

A prestigious music institution affiliated with the Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais in Brazil, offering a wide range of music programs, including undergraduate and graduate degrees. With a rich history dating back to its founding in 1925, it has become a center of musical excellence. The school hosts numerous concerts, recitals, and performances, showcasing the talent of its students and faculty while contributing significantly to the local music scene. The graduation options range from popular instruments, like eletric guitar or drums, to classical ones, like violin or french-horn.
